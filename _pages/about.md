I am a researcher at the **Korea Electronics Technology Institute (KETI)**. My research interests lie at the intersection of AI systems, emerging hardware architectures, and efficient model inference.

My current work focuses on **Processing-in-Memory (PIM)**, **heterogeneous accelerator systems**, and **performance and numerical error modeling for AI workloads**. I study how large language models and diffusion models can be efficiently executed across GPU, TPU, and PIM-based platforms.

I also have research experience in natural language processing, robust text classification, and parameter-efficient model adaptation.

## Research Interests

- Processing-in-Memory and near-memory computing
- Heterogeneous AI systems
- Efficient LLM inference and serving
- AI accelerator modeling and simulation
- Numerical error analysis for AI accelerators
- Efficient diffusion model inference
